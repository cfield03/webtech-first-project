An Introduction to March Madness

This website will provide you a complete guide to enjoy the most hectic week in American sports. Embrace the chaos.

Brief Introduction

March Madness refers to the Championship Week and subsequent NCAA Tournament for basketball that takes place every year during March. In the tournament, 68 teams play each other in a single-elimination shootout. Please click here for a more in depth explanation of the tournament.

About the Site

This site was born out of a personal desire to influence my friends to watch the NCAA Tournament. However, with the amount of teams and games being played it can be overwhelming for new people to start watching. This site will aim to introduce all aspects of the tournament through fun interaction and humorous commentary. So if you have a friend you'd like to get into March Madness please feel free to SEND them the link to this site.

Navigating the Site

The navigation bar above can be used to navigate the website. As a jumping off point here are the lists of all the pages and what content is on the page:

  • History: A complete overview of the history of March Madness and the NCAA Tournament.
  • Bracket: Shows the Tournamnet bracket and allows users to create their own bracket.
  • About: A complete overview of the NCAA Tournament and its terminology.
  • Schedule: Schedule of all upcoming games.
  • Team Supporter: Don't know which team to support? Allow us to choose a random team for you to pull for!
  • Statistics: If you're looking to become a bracket guru, this page will show all important statistics to use while trying to create a perfect bracket.
  • Contact: Create an account with us to stay up to date.
