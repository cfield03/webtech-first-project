Picking your new favorite team

This page will allow you to choose your new favorite team. There are four categories that you can choose your team from.

  1. One-Two Seeds: AKA the best of the best. These are the teams that experts predict are likely to win it all.
  2. Three-Four Seeds: Teams that are also incredibly talented but had some hiccups during the regular season. Liekly to go far or blow it in the first two rounds.
  3. Five-Six Seeds:These teams are solid and have a decent chance at a long run in the tournament.
  4. Seven-Eight Seeds:
    5. Risky picks because there is a 50/50 chance they lose their first game. Otherwise some great runs are possible from these teams.
  5. Nine-Ten Seeds: Some of the most exciting teams in the tournament. They either get blown out in the first round or reach the Final Four, no in between.
  6. Eleven-Twelve Seeds: High risk high reward teams, they've got a solid chance of an exciting first round upset and a run to the Sweet 16.
  7. Thirteen-Fourteen Seeds:High liklihood of not making it very far in the tournament (like not make it out of the first round) but some great characters can be found on these teams if they manage to win.
  8. Fifteen-Sixteen Seeds: High risk low reward chance in this cateogry, high likelihood of losing but on the of chance they manage to win even one game you'll be the happiest person alive.
